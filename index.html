Lee Sang-hyeok (aka Faker)

The true G.O.A.T of League of Legends

Facts about Lee Sang-hyeok's career

  • As of November 2023, he's won 4 League of Legends World Championships making him the only one to do so.
  • He's won two Mid-Season invitationals (2016 and 2017)
  • During his time in the LCK (LoL Champions Korea), he's won 10 titles since 2013
  • He's been with the team of SK T1 since 2013 and has since become part owner in the team.
  • While streaming on Twitch, he broke a record for his first ever livestream of 245,100 viewers
  • He was the first player in the LCK to reach 2,500 kills
  • When and if he retires from competitive gaming, he wants to study. He just isn't sure what subject.
